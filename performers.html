Information for Musicians

Core Memory Music is looking for emerging and established artists to perform in our chamber music series. We want to showcase your artistry and provide our audience with a rich, varied mix of music each season.


Our concerts take place at a private residence in southern Rhode Island, a few miles from the Atlantic coast. The performance space seats up to 30 people and features a Steinway concert grand in excellent condition. Our concerts are informal and we encourage performers to interact with the audience.

All of our concerts take place on Saturday afternoons. Our standard performance fees are $600 for soloists and $500 per artist for duos and trios. We do not reimburse artists for travel expenses but we’re happy to provide ground transportation, meals, and lodging while you’re here.

If this opportunity interests you, send us an email and introduce yourself. Please include your CV and links to a few recent recordings. If you haven’t heard back from us within a week, please send it again — we may have missed your email. Thanks!




Core Memory Music relies on private communications and word of mouth to publicize our activities. Please help us spread the word by sharing this information with interested family and friends.
